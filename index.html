Alan Emtage

I wrote a piece of code that gave birth to a multibillion-dollar industry. I didn't make any money off of it, but I wouldn't change anything.

Biography

Alan Emtage was born on November 27, 1964 in Bridgetown, Barbados. He created Archie, which was the world's first Internet Search Engine.

Achievements

  • 1986: Linked Eastern Canada to the internet for the first time.
  • 1992: Formed Bunyip Information Systems in Montreal, the world's first company that provided Internet information services.
  • 1993: Founded the Internet Society.
  • 1994: Co-chaired the Uniform Resource Identifier working group, creating the standard for Uniform Resource Locators (URLs).
  • 1998: Joined Mediapolis, a web engineering company that has had over hundreds of clients.
  • 2017: Inducted as an Innovator by the Internet Society into the Internet Hall of Fame.
